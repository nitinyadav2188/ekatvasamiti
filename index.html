WHO WE ARE

About Ekatva Samiti

"Ekatva Samiti is a social welfare organization based in Lucknow dedicated to community service, cultural awareness, and public welfare initiatives. The organization actively works to bring people together through charitable programs, community gatherings, educational awareness campaigns, and celebrations of national events.

Our mission is to strengthen social harmony, encourage volunteerism, and create positive change through collective participation.

From organizing Bhandaras and public welfare drives to celebrating Independence Day, Republic Day, and various cultural occasions, Ekatve Samiti continuously strives to serve society with dedication and compassion."

40+ Volunteers Engaged

Our Mission

To uplift underserved sections of society in Lucknow through dedicated food services, national education programs, environment cleanups, and community volunteer mobilization.

Our Vision

To nurture a harmonious, self-reliant, and active community where citizens are motivated by values of nationalism, mutual respect, and selfless community service.

Core Values

  • Ekatva (Unity) & Harmony
  • Selfless Work
  • Cultural Pride & Respect
  • Public Trust & Responsibility
WHAT WE DO

Our Key Activities

01

Bhandara Seva

Our Bhandara Seva represents a significant community outreach endeavor, organized to share nutritional food items and foster high social unity. By welcoming all citizens irrespective of background, we reinforce the traditional Indian value of shared nourishment. Volunteers coordinate sourcing, cooking, and sanitary serving.

Food Distribution Volunteer Support Community Gathering
02

Independence Day Celebration

Every 15th of August, Ekatva Samiti gathers community residents to unfurl the national flag and celebrate our freedom values. We organize singing sessions, distribute sweets, and invite young children to deliver speeches on local freedom fighters. This keeps the torch of independence and civic duty alive.

Flag Ceremony Cultural Programs Public Participation
03

Republic Day Event

Our Republic Day celebrations highlight the value of the Constitution, promoting equality, fundamental rights, and civil responsibilities. Through educational dialogues, interactive quizzes for teenagers, and awards honoring clean local neighborhoods, we inspire positive citizenship.
